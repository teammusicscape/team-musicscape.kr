About the Work

Dawn depicts the moment when the first light of morning gently pushes back darkness. Emerging gradually from silence, rhythm and melody symbolize awakening—both of the day and of the inner self. The music carries a calm yet resilient energy, expressing the strength to rise once more within the stillness of early light.
