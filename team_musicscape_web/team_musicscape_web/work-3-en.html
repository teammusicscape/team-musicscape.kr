About the Work

At the point where light and darkness intertwine, we find ourselves standing on uncertain ground. Amid anxiety and inner conflict, music seeks balance and meaning. Gradually intensifying rhythms and resonances depict the human will to move forward—like navigating powerful waves toward light. This piece stands as a song of encouragement for all who walk through an age of uncertainty.
