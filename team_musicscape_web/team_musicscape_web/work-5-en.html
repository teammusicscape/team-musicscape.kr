About the Work

In a reality marked by war, conflict, corruption, and instability, ordinary people often live with constant unease. Prayer is written for all who continue to endure within such conditions. Rather than a forceful cry, the piece carries its longing through quiet sound, allowing music itself to become an act of prayer.

Small resonances accumulate, forming a fragile yet persistent hope. The work holds the wish that these individual vibrations might gather and remain as a flicker of light within the world—a moment where listening becomes an offering, and sound becomes a space for reflection and care.
