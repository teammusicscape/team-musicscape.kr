About the Work

Composed in response to the Jeju architectural work Stone–Wind–Water by architect Itami Jun (Yoo Dong-ryong), this piece translates architectural philosophy into sound. Inspired by the concept of Cheon–Ji–In (Heaven–Earth–Human) and the elemental presence of wind, stone, and water in Jeju, the music reflects the sensory experience of inhabiting space shaped by nature and thought. The resonance of the guitar evokes these elements, forming a lyrical landscape where architecture, nature, and human spirit intersect.
