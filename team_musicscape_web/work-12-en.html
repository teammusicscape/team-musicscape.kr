About the Work

The path up a mountain is shaped by alternating ascents and descents, calm and difficulty. Passing through deep shade before encountering light again, one begins to see life reflected within the landscape. Mountain translates this journey into music, with the guitar tracing slopes and summits, symbolizing perseverance, achievement, and hope at the end of hardship.
