About the Work

Amid constant competition and external measures of value, we often lose sight of inner calm and small gestures of care. This piece was written from a wish to let quietness and peace resonate from within, rather than be drowned out by external noise. Its initial image came from a walk along a mountain path: a single dandelion blooming alone on a hillside, followed soon after by a slope filled with a wide field of yellow blossoms.

The music traces how a small wish, born within one person, can be carried outward like a seed on the wind—traveling, settling, and taking root elsewhere. The guitar and violin move in close dialogue, surrounding one another as personal resonance opens toward a wider world. As the title suggests, the piece follows a path that extends from 'here' toward 'everywhere,' carrying with it a quiet sense of hope and a message of care that continues beyond its point of origin.
