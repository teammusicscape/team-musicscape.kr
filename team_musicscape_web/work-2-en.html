About the Work

Each time one enters a forest, the trees stand firmly in their own places, revealing a quiet yet vivid presence. Light and wind filtering through pine and nut pine trees, along with the scent of needles, have a way of clearing the mind. Borrowing the imagery of such a forest, this piece is dedicated to those who silently hold their ground and sustain our society in their own places, expressing gratitude and deep respect through sound.
