About the Work

This piece is inspired by the landscapes and encounters of a traveler, and by the moment when one must depart again. Though the journey passes through regions such as Gyeonggi, Jeongseon, Taebaek, and Samcheok, the road becomes a metaphor for life itself. Meetings and partings, joy and sorrow, unfold along the way. Melodies in the Menari mode carry regional sensibilities, while the dialogue between piri and guitar brings together tradition and modernity, creating a new resonance. The familiar yet unfamiliar soundscape invites listeners to reflect on their own journeys, offering quiet consolation and insight.
