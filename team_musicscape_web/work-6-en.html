About the Work

This piece draws inspiration from the breath, brushwork, and spirit of traditional Korean calligraphy. Each section unfolds like a continuous brushstroke, transforming guitar techniques into musical equivalents of ink density and gestural energy. Calligraphy was composed in connection with a special exhibition on Choi Chi-won at the Seoul Arts Center Calligraphy Museum, following research and site visits conducted with artists from various disciplines. The work stands as a tribute to Korean calligraphic tradition and to the life and philosophy of Choi Chi-won, reflecting the focused breath and upright posture of scholars who prepared both body and mind before writing.
